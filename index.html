Definitions

Intersectionality

"A way of understanding and analyzing the complexity in the world, in people, and in human experiences"

– Patricia Hill Collins and Sirma Bilge from Intersectionality

Interaction Design (IxD)

"The structure and behavior of interactive systems"

– The Interaction Design Association

Design Justice

"Rethinks design processes, centers people who are normally marginalized by design, and uses collaborative, creative practices to address the deepest challenges our communities face"

– The Design Justice Network
